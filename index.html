About me



I'm a gamer turned game developer. Mainly work in Unity, and worked with Unreal engine in free time. Game design and system mechanics are my favourite part of game development, with close favourite being creating AI opponents. Strategy games, especially turn-based, are my favourite gaming genre, which may coincide with why I like making complex systems, with branching options, and almost limitless opportunities.



Projects

Diableros



One of my favourite projects, and the one I worked on the longest. 3D top-down rougelike shooter, inspired by Diablo and Archero. It is a project where I learned most about game development and Unity.




Last Stand



A revisioning of Diableros, project where I became lead developer, going through the hurdles the position throws at you. Learned a lot about the managing process of game development, importance of minor details, and their further causality.




Last Survivors



Vampire Survivors inspired version of Last Stand. Quite a challenge in terms of peformance, but one I was able to overcome, and have a screen full of enemies, as expected from such a game genre.






Football Clash Arena





First game to have worked on. Beautiful combination of football and chess, with simultanious turns. The favourite part were the AI opponents that I made, fooling many people into believing they played real people.
