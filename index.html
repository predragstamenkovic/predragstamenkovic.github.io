Vampire Survivors inspired version of Last Stand. Quite a challenge in terms of peformance. Unlike Vampire Survivors and other clones, using 2D sprites for their units, we tried with 3D. Jumping from the usual 20~ish opponents to over hundred, while also trying not to make mobile phones cry, was something that took a good amount of work. Through model and texture optimizations, learning ECS and DOTS, using the threading capabilities, and then transfering half of the project to the new system, the goal was achieved. It is a shame the store didn't see the project in the end.

Engine:  Unity
Company: Advenworks
